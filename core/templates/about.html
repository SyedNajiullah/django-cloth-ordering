ABOUT US

2Men is the definition of luxury Italian fashion - and aims to bring the absolute best brands and products together under one roof.

ANDREA TARANTINO

Communication & Marketing

Born in Neapolitan tradition, Andrea brings decades of experience in the luxury clothing industry. He is the voice of 2Men, ensuring our message of sartorial excellence reaches gentlemen worldwide.

GIUSEPPE MOTTOLA

Shipping & Operations

Giuseppe ensures that every package leaving our store in Aversa reaches its destination with the care and speed that luxury products deserve. He manages the logistics that make global Neapolitan style possible.

CIRO

Chief Designer

Ciro is the creative soul of 2Men. With a deep understanding of Neapolitan tailoring, he curates our collections to ensure only the highest quality handmade products are offered to our discerning clients.

The idea is to give those who want to shop at the highest level a place to securely view, choose, and buy with absolute confidence.
